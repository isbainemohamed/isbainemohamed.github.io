Project details

In Our days, people use social media networks with a unbelievable frequency, writing posts, sharing photos and videos and sending private or public messages. One of th most used social network is Tweeter. Twitter is one of the most popular social media platforms in the world, with 330 million monthly active users and 500 million tweets sent each day. Since Tweeter generate a huge amount of data (6000 tweets per second). In this project, we will build a model that can tell us what sentiment is expressed in a given tweet.
